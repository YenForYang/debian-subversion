Revisions and Peg Revisions.

A brief word on operative and peg revisions.

If the kind of the peg revision is svn_opt_revision_unspecified, then it defaults to svn_opt_revision_head for URLs and svn_opt_revision_working for local paths.

For deeper insight, please see the Peg and Operative Revisions section of the Subversion Book.
